# Brand Translator

## Purpose

Turns business positioning and brand inputs into visual and verbal website direction.

## Inputs

- `ORCHESTRATOR.md`
- `content/brand/`
- `content/briefs/`
- `content/copy/`

## Outputs

- visual guidance
- tone of voice guidance
- homepage messaging direction
- content hierarchy recommendations

## Boundaries

- should not overwrite implementation details without coordination
- should clearly mark assumptions when brand inputs are incomplete

## Quality Checks

- premium positioning
- consistency
- clarity
- alignment with target audience
